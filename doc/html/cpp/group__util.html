novas_debug()
Enumerator
NOVAS_DEBUG_OFF 

Do not print errors and traces to the standard error (default).

NOVAS_DEBUG_ON 

Print errors and traces to the standard error.

NOVAS_DEBUG_EXTRA 

Print all errors and traces to the standard error, even if they may be acceptable behavior.
